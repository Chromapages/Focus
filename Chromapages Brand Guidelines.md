## **1\) Visual Identity System: "Illuminated Precision"**

### **1.1 The Core Aesthetic**

Our look is defined by **clarity, light, and depth.** We avoid heavy dark modes for marketing content, preferring a clean, editorial look.

* **Light Glassmorphism:** UI elements use white translucency (bg-white/60, backdrop-blur-md) to float above the Stardust background.  
* **Colored Shadows:** Instead of dark drop shadows, we use **colored glows** (shadow-indigo-500/20) to create a "lifted" feel.  
* **Contrast:** Dark, heavy typography anchored on light backgrounds creates immediate authority.

### **1.3 Typography System (Unchanged)**

We use a high-contrast pairing of "Massive Editorial" and "Technical Precision."

* **Headlines (H1/Hero):** **Montserrat ExtraBold (800)**.  
  * *Usage:* Massive scale, tight tracking (\-0.02em).  
  * *Color:* Deep Indigo or Void.  
* **Section Titles (H2/H3):** **Montserrat ExtraBold (800)**.  
  * *Usage:* Card titles, section headers.  
* **Data & Labels:** **JetBrains Mono**.  
  * *Usage:* KPIs (\+40%), tags (FINTECH).  
  * *Purpose:* Signals engineering precision.  
* **Body Copy:** **Inter**.  
  * *Usage:* Long-form text.

### **1.4 UI Components**

#### **Buttons ("The Prism")**

* **Structure:** Deep Indigo fill \+ Teal border accent.  
* **Hover:** Lifts up (translate-y-1), shadow expands (shadow-xl).  
* **Text:** White, Montserrat Bold (700), uppercase.

#### **Cards ("The Lens")**

* **Default:** White glass (bg-white/60), thin border (border-white/40).  
* **Hover:** Becomes opaque white (bg-white), border shifts to Teal.  
* **Shadow:** Soft, diffuse colored shadow (shadow-brand-primary/10).
